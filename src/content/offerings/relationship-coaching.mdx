So you met someone cute at a party. Did you talk to her — or rehearse the perfect opener until she left?

If that's familiar, you're in the right place. I coach single men who want real connection but freeze when it's time to actually approach. Rejection feels unbearable. You worry about coming off as creepy. And honestly, you're not even sure what you want from your romantic life.

I get it, because I've been there.

## Who this is for

You want to connect with women. You're tired of overthinking every interaction. You don't want tricks. You want to become someone for whom talking to people you're drawn to is just a normal part of the week.

**Who it's not for:** guys looking for manipulation tactics, scripts to "win" women, or a coach who'll promise you a girlfriend by week six. I'm NOT training pick-up artists.

## The promise

Three months from now, you:

- **Approache regularly.** Talking to women you're drawn to is a normal part of your week, not a once-a-year act of courage.
- **Handle rejection without spiraling.** A "no" stings for a minute, not a month. You know your worth doesn't ride on any single interaction.
- **Know what you wants and can voice it.** You're clear on what you're looking for, and you can express it without apology or pressure.

I can't promise outcomes with any particular woman — nobody honest can. What I promise is training: reps, support, and honest feedback, every week.

## What you get

- **12 weekly 1:1 sessions** (60 min, video) — a somatic check-in, a review of your week's reps, one main focus, and a clear next step.
- **Weekly field challenges** — real-world reps, scaled to where you are. Early on, that might be making eye contact and smiling at five people. Later, it might be collecting five rejections on purpose. Rejection is training, not failure.
- **Between-session support** — voice and text access, for the moment right after a hard rejection or right before a scary approach.
- **Personalized practices** — self-compassion and nervous-system tools so your confidence lives in your body, not just your head.
- **Frameworks that stick** — clear ways to figure out what you actually want, plus a grounded picture of how women commonly experience being approached — built from real stories from women, not manosphere theory.
