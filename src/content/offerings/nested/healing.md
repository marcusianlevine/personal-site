It took me many years to grapple with the reality that I had deep wounds from my childhood and early relationships that would not resolve on their own. For the past 5 years, I have been on a journey of healing and growth, working with therapists, coaches, and healers across a wide range of modalities to address these wounds. I am now in a place where I can help others on similar journeys.

In total, I've tried over 50 different healing modalities - ranging from traditional talk therapy to psychedelic-assisted therapy, from family constellations (with people and with horses) to reiki energy healing, from mindfulness practices to breathwork and sound healing. I believe that different people resonate with different modalities, and I certainly cannot help everyone with the modalities that I offer.

I am currently offering:
- Intuitive bodywork (in-person)
- Guided Daoist inner alchemy meditations (in-person or online)

If you're interested in exploring any of these healing modalities, reach out to me. I travel full-time so if you're interested in an in-person modality, make sure to include your location in the message.
